Review Essays
Contact Ara for Essay Review Service
Each top global University wants to know a student better and his/her life’s journey along with learnings therefrom; before admitting him/her.

The essays that these universities want students to write as part of their application have proven to be an effective method to know what motivates a student and/or what has inspired a student and/or what has made the student that he/she is. That is, such essays are a window for the universities to peep into a student’s life, personality and character. Its their way to hear a student’s story – to know the student as well as possible. These essays very often become the final decision point for most universities to accept or reject an application.

We help our students to brain storm and ideate on what they can present in their essays. Our focus is to enable our students to articulate their thoughts; that is, tell their story better and more effectively.

Ara's support in Essay Reviews includes in-line feedback, suggesting edits, language changes (for Tone, Mood and overall Expressions) and Structure (Start, Paragraphs, Transition, Continuity and Closure).


It is always better to get an "outsider's" perspective to your application and/or essays. We help you do that with our exclusive Application Review Service and Essay Review Service.

Feedback: You may submit your draft essays and we will provide feedback on how you can improve your articulation and/or further develop your thoughts and ideas.

Distinctiveness: Ara's experts use their experience to identify what is unique about you vis-a-vis other qualified Students. We will provide guidance so that you can articulate your story in your own distinctive way.

Language: In seconds, University Admissions Officers accept or reject an application based on how the Essay is articulated. Ara's experts help you by providing per/in-line feedback to improve the overall presentation of your essay.
