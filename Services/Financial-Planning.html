Financial Planning




World's top Universities are expensive to study in.

Well, education cost is one of the biggest expenditures we incur in our life (after buying or building a house). Having said that, we don't call it an expenditure. It is an investment! With a very high ROI (Return on Investment)!

The total cost of studying at such Universities is called 'Cost of Attendance'. This includes Tuition, Stay, Food, Insurance and some living expenses. However, this Cost of Attendance does not include travel cost, other costs (of living) that are not related to the University. This also does not include the cost incurred at the time of applications and the 'shifting/moving' cost.
